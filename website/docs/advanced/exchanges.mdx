SwiftGraphQLClient is centered around the idea of exchanges. Exchanges are a natural way to model the flow of continuous data. Multiple exchanges form a pipeline where each exchange only does one job and leaves the rest to other exchanges. An exchange may either modify an operation (e.g. `AuthExchange`, `LogExchange`), process an operation (e.g. `FetchExchange`, `WebSocketExchange`) or both (e.g. `CacheExchange`).
